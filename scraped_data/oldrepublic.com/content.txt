Managing Old Republic for the Long Run
Our MISSION is to provide quality insurance security and related services to businesses, individuals, and public institutions, and be a dependable long-term steward of the trust that policyholders, shareholders, and other important stakeholders place in us.
Old Republic International Corporation (ORI) is a leading specialty insurer that operates diverse property & casualty and title insurance companies. Founded in 1923 and a member of the Fortune 500, we are a leader in underwriting and risk management services for business partners across the United States and Canada. Our specialized operating companies are experts in their fields, enabling us to provide tailored solutions that set us apart.
Old Republic operates in a decentralized manner that emphasizes specialization by type of insurance coverage and industry, ensuring broad diversification and dispersion of risk. ORI’s performance reflects an entrepreneurial spirit, a necessary long-term orientation, and a corporate culture that promotes integrity and accountability.
